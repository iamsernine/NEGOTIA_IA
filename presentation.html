Architecture

Agentic deal desk with business controls

Negotia AI Orchestrator

Routes the conversation and coordinates tools.

Lead Qualification Engine

Extracts budget, authority, timeline, and seriousness.

Inventory / Offer Tool

Finds public product options without exposing private fields.

Pricing Strategy Tool

Recommends safe public counters and value levers.

Payment + Trade-In Tools

Produces non-binding estimates and verification needs.

Deal Integrity Layer

Blocks private pricing extraction and unsafe final approval.

Brief Generator

Creates a human-ready Initial Negotiation Brief.

ADK Web Front

Live interface from the GitHub developer app files.

Human Final Review

Final commercial decisions stay accountable.
